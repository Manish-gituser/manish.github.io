# about

About me

I am a Data Engineer with nearly 5 years of experience in designing, building,
and optimizing scalable data pipelines for analytics and business intelligence
use cases.

I specialize in Apache Spark, PySpark, Spark SQL, Databricks, Delta Lake, and
cloud platforms such as AWS and Microsoft Azure. I have hands-on experience
developing ETL and ELT pipelines, implementing data quality checks, and
optimizing performance.

Currently working as a Data Engineer at Genpact, I focus on building robust,
reliable, and scalable data solutions that support data-driven decision-making.
